002 - when you love someone

The act of loving is like revealing your skin bare, vulnerable; and to receive love when the beating heart has been wounded before, either by a person or by the happenings in the world, is like believing someone with a knife won't stab you in the heart.

But would you believe me if I told you that I had met someone who doesn't carry a knife around? He is like a walking peace; The Prince of Peace, and his name is Jesus Christ and his words will give you light, peace, and joy. He is the way, the truth, and the life.
