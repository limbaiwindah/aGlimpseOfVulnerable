001 - so fragile

The relationships between humans are so fragile that they can be broken by the slightest shift in the wind of life. We express our adoration only to wish later we did not; we love our best only then to hate that we cared that much.

But that's the thing about humans, that's the thing about life. We don't know if it's permanent or not, so maybe it's the heaven above we should look up to; and maybe it's not human to cling to, but the one who is worthy to be called "my everything".
